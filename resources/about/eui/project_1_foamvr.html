DIY foamboard VR device

This VR device is completely cardboard compatibe, though currently my phone sucks too hard for it to be a viable approach.

View from face side, showing the large eyepiece.

This device really requires you to have a 5.5-inch device, of which I dont have, to give enough field of view to not be annoyingly limited.

Inside the eyepiece, showing moderate amount of distortion and limited FoV from the 4.7-inch screen.

The device, in it's current configuration, allows a user to see VR content before investing in expensive systemms auch as the HTC vive or the oculus rift.

It can also be used to watch 3D movies, though at a resolution of 480x272 (the same as that of a PSP) the picture quality is adequate at best. That said, If you have a 1080p or better 5.5inch device, this can be immensely mitigated.
