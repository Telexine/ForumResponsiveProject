Simple Wireless Internet Bridge

This Internet Bridge is simple to install, simple to set up, and performs flawlessly for my needs.

The bridge itself regulary scores 75mbps+ in speed tests, letting me use the full potential of the 100mbps fiber connection at the other end.

The Ubiquiti NanoStation loco M5 trancievers accept 100mbps ethernet on either end, and once connected, is fully transparent to either side of the network.

The setting up process is painless, the only hard part is to align the two transcievers. This process needs to be done with the utmost precision, lest the connection quality drops out.

These transcievers are not designed to be used indoors, especially through at least 50 inches of concrete. However that only means that the alignment becomes crucial, due to the signal strength of high-frequency waves passing through matter. Once aligned the product works as advertised.
